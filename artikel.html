UPDATE (November 19, 2020): Shortly after noting his eligibility for the 2020 NBA Draft as he has been in training camps and in talks with teams, Sheck Wes released a new track fittingly titled, “2020 DRAFT.” Accompanied by a music video, the visual sees Ronnie 2K, Zack Bia, John O, Moe Black, Mok Lite, Father Steve, Cian Moore, Kidthewiz and Sturdy Max taking part in the playful video. Sheck is seen in training mode, “showtiming” in subway cars, dribbling on the courts and more.
